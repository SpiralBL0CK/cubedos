\chapter{Development}
\label{chapt:development}

This chapter describes the internal design of CubedOS. It is of interest to developers who wish
to extend the functionality of CubedOS or users who want to better understand the system's
internal architecture.

This chapter is broken into several sections. The first section describes the requirements that
CubedOS is intended to meet. The second section describes the overall architecture of CubedOS.
The third section provides a detailed design of the CubedOS core modules.
